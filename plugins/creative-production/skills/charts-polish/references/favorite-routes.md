## Route Selection

- For partner-facing or executive use, start with Executive Visual One-Pager, Comparison Proof Card, Minimalist, or Incredible Keynote.
- For creative exploration, use Creative Learning Memo, Product Proof Storyboard, UGC Hook Opening Frame, Channel Format Wall, Drawing Style, 3D Style, Chart With Characters, or Creative Style.
- For website or campaign visuals, use Landing Page Hero Concept, UGC Hook Opening Frame, Channel Format Wall, or Incredible Keynote.
